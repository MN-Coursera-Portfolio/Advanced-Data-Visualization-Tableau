PART 5: LOD AND COMPLEX CALCULATIONS
Task 1: Price Difference (LOD)
Formula:
[Price] - { FIXED [Neighbourhood] : AVG([Price]) }
Explanation: This calculation subtracts the average price of all listings in a specific neighbourhood from the price of the individual listing, identifying which listings are priced above or below their local average.

Task 2: Total Revenue
Formula:
[Price] * [Minimum Nights]
Explanation: Estimates the potential revenue per booking based on the minimum required stay.

Task 3: Occupancy Rate
Formula:
[Availability 365] / 365
Explanation: Calculates the proportion of the year the listing is available, serving as a proxy for occupancy potential.
